Hybrid Workforce in the Healthcare Sector

The healthcare sector is one of the prominent sectors to adapt to cutting-edge AI innovations to boost the productivity of doctors and nurses. The hybrid workforce involves the interaction between humans and hi-tech machines for detecting diseases, tumours and other medical conditions at a very early stage. The applications help the doctors with highly accurate reports of patients and generate alerts for emergencies. AI and machine learning algorithms leverage real-time data of patients and predict the outcomes efficiently to save lives daily. Surgical robots with VR and AR functionalities are guiding medical students to have virtual operations on virtual patients.
